About Me

Hi, I’m a 17-year-old photographer with a passion for finding the extraordinary in the everyday. Based in the Czech Republic, I specialize in motosport and car photography, blending a youthful perspective with a technical eye for detail. For me, photography isn't just about taking pictures—it’s about freezing a feeling and sharing a story that words might miss. Whether I’m chasing the perfect "golden hour" light or capturing the raw energy of the city, I strive to create images that feel authentic and timeless.
